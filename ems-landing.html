How It Works
Built to Get You Certified
Not flashcards. A full adaptive system that finds your weaknesses and closes them before test day.
🎯
Adaptive Difficulty
Questions adjust in real time based on your performance. Nail the basics and get pushed into harder clinical reasoning — struggle and it backs off to rebuild your foundation.
🔥
Weak Areas Mode
After every session the app tracks what you missed. Weak Areas mode fires only questions from your problem zones — no wasting time on what you already know cold.
📖
Real Explanations
Every question — right or wrong — shows a detailed clinical explanation. Not just "the answer is B." You understand the why so you can apply it on any question, any day.
🎲
Mixed Exam Mode
Simulate the real NREMT with randomized 30-question exams pulled from all 6 categories at mixed difficulty — as close to test-day conditions as you can get.
📊
Performance Breakdown
Every results screen breaks down your score by category so you see exactly where you stand — and where to spend the next study session.
📱
Study Anywhere
Works on your phone, tablet, or laptop. No app to install — open it in any browser. Study on break, between calls, or the night before your exam.
